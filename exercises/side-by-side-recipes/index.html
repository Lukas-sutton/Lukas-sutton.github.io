FRENCH-STYLE MASHED POTATOES (POMMES PURÉE)

PREP TIME:20m COOK TIME:20m Total Time:3 YIELDS:6 SERVINGS

These French-style mashed potatoes, or pommes purée, are the silkiest, most luxurious version of mashed potatoes you’ll ever try. Yukon Golds are cooked whole, riced, then mixed with cold butter and warm cream before being passed through a fine sieve for that signature French smoothness. Finished with a pat of butter, flaky sea salt, and fresh chives, they’re pure comfort in every bite.
